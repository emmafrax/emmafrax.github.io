MASTER THESIS

UNDER CONSTRUCTION: I am currently working on my master thesis with Professor Vicenç Gómez , which is about multi-agent reinforcement learning. In particular, I am analyzing a state-of-the-art algorithm and finding possible ways to improve its performance and understanding in the context of a line of research developed within the AI&ML group (UPF). I am analyzing whether analytical properties of value functions, such as linear decomposability or monotonicity, are required to solve some particular practical multi-agent problems, in the framework of entropy-regularized MDPs.
